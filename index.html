About Me. I love technology.

Since I was a young girl playing with Barbies, I found ways to implement electronics / technology into my play. I taped single bulbs taken out of a Christmas light strip to the tops of each floor of my Barbie-doll house, connecting them in seiries with small alligator-clip wires and a 4-pack of AA batteries. Ever since, I've been driven to create and enhance things to become more lively, of course, using electronics. This drive soon led me to my hobby of microcontroller programming, and it's become a beneficial venture to say the least. Being able to understand the electronics and functionality of technology in the home has given me the power and ability to repair what most would have to replace. This too, has allowed me the freedom to create new things from old things, and inspire and teach others to do the same.

And lastly, I'm thirsty for more.

Never has a day gone by where I haven't thought of another idea to create something, somehow, someway. Whether it's my other hobbies of sewing / jewelry making, or gardening/landscaping, I have an ever-flowing source of creative ideas to implement some day.
